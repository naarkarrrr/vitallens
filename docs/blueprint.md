# **App Name**: MediTrack Pro

## Core Features:

- Patient Management: Create, read, update, and delete patient records including personal information, medical history, and contact details using Firestore.
- Encounter Management: Log and track patient encounters, including visit details, departments, and attending doctors.
- Vitals Monitoring: Record and display patient vital signs such as temperature, heart rate, and blood pressure during encounters using Firestore.
- Diagnosis and Lab Results: Store and manage patient diagnoses and lab results, linking them to specific encounters.
- Prescription Management: Create and manage patient prescriptions, including drug names, dosages, and frequencies using Firestore.
- Bed Management: Track bed availability, assign patients to beds, and manage bed statuses within the hospital.
- AI-Powered Clinical Recommendations: Generate clinical recommendations based on patient data. This feature will incorporate a reasoning tool that determines when recommendations are relevant.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft blue (#64B5F6) to convey trust and calmness.
- Background color: Light gray (#F5F5F5), offering a clean and professional look. Desaturated by about 25% and bright for the light scheme.
- Accent color: Light green (#A5D6A7) to highlight important actions and notifications. Hue is approximately 30 degrees to the "left" of soft blue.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif offering a modern, machined, objective, neutral look, suitable for both headlines and body text
- Use clear, consistent icons to represent different modules and functions within the system.
- Employ a side navigation and top header for easy access to different sections.
- Use subtle transitions and animations to enhance user experience and guide interactions.
